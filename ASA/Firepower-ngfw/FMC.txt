FMC Firepower management center can be used to manage all the FTD's Firepower threat defence.
From the Firepower mgmt center you can create policies, licences, monitoring etc. 

We as the admin connect to the FMC and the FMC connects to the FTD 
FTD run on 2100,4100,9300 appliances. They can also be virtual appliances hosted in cloud platforms
or vsphere. 

FMC can be physical or virtual.
